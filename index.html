About The Play

For ages, the birds enjoyed a safe homeland. Families lived together in harmony, a stringent yet emphatic king ruled over them and daily bread was earned and enjoyed. Values were strict and sages would multiply each day, but a respectable social order was maintained.
Fast forward a couple years, the new age birds seem to be weakening the construction of the pigeons’ society. Economy starts to fall, social ties break and instabilities emerge. The Supreme pigeon is chaotic and so is his kingdom; chaotic enough to be impervious to an enemy.
A hippie crow has found his way in, on his ethereal quest to become one of the pigeons. Watch this epic drama unfold with music to immerse yourself in. You don’t want to miss the flyest event of your life
